🎆 July 4th Fireworks — You're in the Right Place

This year's competition ends on Independence Day, which means you're in one of the best spots in the country to watch fireworks. Stand on the beach and you can see shows launching in both directions along the coast.

  • Myrtle Beach Boardwalk — 9:00 PM, fireworks launched from 2nd Avenue Pier, free, best viewed from the beach
  • Cherry Grove Pier (North Myrtle Beach) — 9:30 PM, 15 min north
  • Barefoot Landing — 10:00 PM, over the lake, 15 min north
  • Broadway at the Beach — 10:00 PM, over Lake Broadway, 10 min away

Note: The regular Wednesday fireworks at the boardwalk are moved to Saturday July 4th this year. Expect crowds — arrive early for a good spot on the sand.

☀️ Pack serious sunscreen. South Carolina summer sun is intense — much stronger than the Northeast. Pack more than you think you'll need.
